1. Payment Policy

All projects and template orders require payment confirmation before delivery or project commencement.

2. Refund Policy

All payments are final. Due to the digital nature of our products and services. we do not offer refunds once delivery has been made.

3. Delivery Timeline

Delivery timelines begin after payment confirmation and may vary based on project scope. Templates are delivered within 12-24 hours after verification

4. Client Responsibility

Clients are responsible for providing accurate information and required assets. Delays in submission may affect delivery timelines.

5. Communication Policy

All official communication is conducted via email. We do not guarantee responses outside business hours

6. Revisions (for services)

Revisions are limited and defined per project scope. Additional changes outside the agreed scope may incur extra fees.

7. Ownership & Usage

Upon full payment, clients receive ownership of the final deliverables. We reserve the right to showcase work for portfolio purposes unless otherwise agreed.

8. Payment Verification

Orders are processed only after payment verification. Incomplete or unverifiable transactions may be declined.

9. Fraud Protection

We reserve the right to reject any order suspected of fraudulent or invalid payment activity
